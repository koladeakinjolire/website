About Me

I am a curious, driven individual whose passions span soccer, basketball, entertainment, finance, and healthy living. With a keen analytical mind, I thrive at the intersection of numbers and narratives, using data to uncover insights that empower people and organizations to make smarter, more informed decisions. Whether analyzing player performance on the pitch, team performance on the basketball court or breaking down trends in the market, I enjoy bringing a deep sense of purpose and precision to my work.

As a lifelong learner, I dedicate much of my free time to reading across genres, constantly seeking fresh perspectives and tools to sharpen my thinking. I also stay active through regular workouts, swimming, and pick-up games of basketball and soccer, which fuel my appreciation for high-level athletic performance and strategy. My love for media spans film, podcasts, music, and sports coverage, offering both relaxation and inspiration.

My favorite projects are those that blend my diverse interests with the power of analytics. My ability to translate complex data into carefully narrated stories as well as actionable insights sets me apart, and I aim to continue contributing to fields where smart decisions make a meaningful difference.
